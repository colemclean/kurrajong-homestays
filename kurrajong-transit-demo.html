How close is a suburb to campus?

Kurrajong only lists homes within an hour's public transport of your campus. Try a Gold Coast suburb to see the indicative commute to Bond and Griffith. Because suburbs are large, this is a range, not a door-to-door figure — the live product computes the real time from each home's exact address.

These are indicative ranges for the suburb as a whole. A real commute depends on the home's exact address and nearest stop — two homes in the same suburb can differ by 20+ minutes. Kurrajong measures each listing individually.
How the real product works. For every listing, Kurrajong geocodes the host's precise address and computes the public-transport time door-to-campus once (via a transit routing service), refreshing it periodically — so what a student sees is that home's real commute, not a suburb average, and the search stays fast. The figures here are illustrative weekday-morning estimates for the demo only.
