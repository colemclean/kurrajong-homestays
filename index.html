Questions

Good to know.

How is Kurrajong different from a homestay agency?

We don't charge weekly management fees or a list of surcharges. Students pay a flat 5% of rent, hosts pay 5%, and a refundable deposit holds a placement — never charged unless you go ahead. We also show the real public-transport commute to campus and only list homes within an hour.

Who can use Kurrajong?

Adults 18+ — including international students, interstate students and working holiday makers — on the Gold Coast. We currently serve Bond University, Griffith University (Gold Coast) and nearby work locations, with more to come.

How do the police checks work?

Both hosts and students hold a current National Police Check (ordered directly from the AFP, about $56), and we re-check every year. Your exact address and contact details stay private until both sides are verified.

How much do hosts earn?

It depends on meals and room, but full-board placements for over-18 students are typically around $350 a week (2026 rates). Hosting up to two students is generally tax-free — please confirm with the ATO.

When are you launching?

We're onboarding our first Gold Coast hosts and students now, aiming for placements from the January intake. Join the waitlist and you'll be first to know.
