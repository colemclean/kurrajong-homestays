Common questions

How is Nestbys different from a homestay agency?svg>
div>
No weekly management fees or surcharges. Students pay a flat 5% of rent, hosts pay 5%, and a refundable deposit holds a placement. We show the real commute to campus and only list homes within an hour.
div>
div>
Who can use Nestbys?svg>
div>
Adults 18+ — including international students, interstate students and working holiday makers — on the Gold Coast. We serve Bond University, Griffith University (Gold Coast) and nearby work locations.
div>
div>
How do the police checks work?svg>
div>
Both hosts and students hold a current National Police Check (from the AFP, about $56), re-checked every year. Your address stays private until both sides are verified.
div>
div>
How much do hosts earn?svg>
div>
Full-board placements are typically around A$350 a week (2026 rates). Hosting up to two students is generally tax-free — confirm with the ATO.
div>
div>

div>
We're onboarding our first Gold Coast hosts, students and working holiday makers now, aiming for placements from the January 2027 intake. Join the waitlist to be first to know.
