# Kurrajong Homestays

Safe, verified homestays for international and interstate students on the Gold Coast, QLD.

**Live site:** https://colemclean.github.io/kurrajong-homestays/

## What it is

Kurrajong Homestays matches adult (18+) international and interstate students with ID-verified, police-checked Gold Coast host families within 60 minutes' public transport of Bond University and Griffith University's Gold Coast campus, for stays of four months or more.

**Model:** Students and hosts each pay a flat 5% of monthly rent. A refundable A$35 deposit hold replaces the industry-standard non-refundable ~A$350 placement fee.
